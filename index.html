Incentive Alignment

Portfolio Performance Fee

Nova Wealth earns a performance fee only when your portfolio grows. This fee is charged on net portfolio growth, calculated after deducting all fees and excluding new contributions.

Performance Fee Formula

Performance Fee = Rate × (Closing Value − Opening Value − Net New Contributions)

No portfolio growth in a given period = zero performance fee payable.
